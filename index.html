Our Story

Five years ago, María was a very happy bunny living her beloved U.K., sipping pints, reading books, and jumping into puddles with her newly discovered wellies. Meanwhile, Garen was travelling the world, discovering Mexican coffee and drinking Danish beer. The two met on the advice of a magpie in Edinburgh, and have been together since.

Details

The wedding will occur in México--specifically, Valle de Guadalupe, an area ninety minutes south (by car) of San Diego. The area is known for its wineries.

We're still fine tuning the details, including providing transportation and detailed instructions for cross the border. We´ll be sending out invitations by post as soon as we can. However, we can guarantee good food, great wine, and amazing company!
